📊 Industrial Process Performance Analytics Dashboard:

A Power BI–based industrial analytics project designed to monitor and analyze operational performance across key plant systems including P-Boiler quality monitoring, Ash Handling Plant efficiency, and Coal Feed energy performance.

The project focuses on data-driven operational insights, enabling engineers and plant operators to identify performance issues, optimize fuel quality, and improve process efficiency through interactive dashboards.

🏭 Project Overview:

Industrial plants generate large volumes of operational data related to fuel quality, ash handling, and boiler performance.
However, this data is often underutilized due to lack of structured visualization and analysis.

This project transforms raw industrial datasets into interactive Power BI dashboards that help stakeholders monitor performance, detect inefficiencies, and perform root cause analysis.

The solution includes three analytical dashboards:

1️⃣ P-Boiler TOC Quality & Sample Turnaround Time Analysis
2️⃣ Ash Handling Plant (AHP) Performance & TSS Analysis
3️⃣ Coal Feed Quality & Energy Performance – Root Cause Analysis

📁 Dashboard Modules

1️⃣ P-Boiler TOC Quality & Sample Turnaround Time Analysis

This dashboard analyzes boiler sample quality and laboratory processing efficiency.

Key Metrics:

Minimum TOC value

Maximum TOC value

Minimum sample processing time

Maximum sample processing time

Key Analysis:

TOC trend analysis by year

Shift-wise sample processing performance

Average result turnaround time by year and shift

Sample count distribution across years

Business Insight

Helps identify:

Slow laboratory processing

Quality deviations in boiler samples

Operational differences across plant shifts


2️⃣ Ash Handling Plant (AHP) Performance & TSS Analysis

This dashboard monitors ash slurry quality and suspended solids behavior in the ash handling system.

Key Metrics:

Minimum TSS value

Maximum TSS value

Maximum output duration

Minimum output duration

Key Analysis:

TSS variation vs output duration

Time-based TSS trend analysis

Sample source comparison (AWS, CTS, HB, HS)

Business Insight

Helps identify:

Process instability in ash handling

High suspended solids levels

Operational impact of output duration on slurry quality


3️⃣ Coal Feed Quality & Energy Performance Analysis

This dashboard evaluates coal fuel quality and its impact on energy output.

Key Metrics:

Average GCV (Gross Calorific Value)

Average Ash %

Average Total Moisture %

Average Fixed Carbon %

Average Inherent Moisture %

Key Analysis:

Correlation analysis

Ash vs GCV

Moisture vs GCV

Fixed Carbon vs GCV

Bunker-wise coal quality comparison

Interactive filtering by date, shift, and sample

Business Insight

Helps identify:

Root causes of low energy output

Impact of ash and moisture on fuel efficiency

Variations in coal quality across bunkers
